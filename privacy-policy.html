Privacy Policy

Ouna Academic Planner

Updated April 12, 2026

At Ouna, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Account Information: Name and email when you create an account.
  • Profile Photos: Profile pictures you choose to upload.
  • Planner Content: Tasks, schedules, courses, and any content you add.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, session duration, and interaction patterns.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize our academic planning services
  • To sync your data across your devices using iCloud
  • To improve and optimize your experience
  • To communicate with you about app updates and support

📸 Photo Library Access

Ouna uses Apple PhotosUI to let you select profile pictures. We only access photos you explicitly choose. Your photo library is never uploaded to our servers—all images remain on your device.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data lives in your personal iCloud and is accessible only to you.

Third-Party Services

Children's Privacy

Ouna does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time through the app settings.
